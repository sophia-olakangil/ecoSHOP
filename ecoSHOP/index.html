Welcome to ecoSHOP!




Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce the carbon footprint of our community by streamlining eco-friendly grocery options and promoting sustainable shopping habits. We believe that small changes in our daily routines can have a big impact on the environment, and grocery shopping is a great place to start. By identifying products from environmentally responsible suppliers, we aim to make it easy and accessible for people to reduce their carbon emissions through their daily shopping choices. Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond just our products, as we also strive to minimize waste and promote circular packaging solutions. Join us in creating a cleaner and healthier planet, one grocery purchase at a time.

Carbon Emission Levels

ecoSHOP measures the carbon emission level of each product using three levels. The lower the carbon emission level of a product, the better it is for the environment. Each level is worth a certain amount of ecoPoints. The more points you obtain through eco-friendly purchase, the closer you get to earning rewards for future grocery purchases!



LOW - 10 points
MEDIUM - 5 points
HIGH - 0 points
